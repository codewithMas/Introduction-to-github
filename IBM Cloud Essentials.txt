IBM Cloud Essentials
About this Course
This course introduces you to the IBM Cloud. 
You will learn about the many offerings and services on IBM Cloud that make it the most open and secure public cloud for developers and enterprises. 
The course begins with an introduction to the IBM Cloud platform which covers topics such as data center locations and configuring identity and access management. 
You will discover the various Infrastructure-as-a-Service (IaaS) options available on IBM Cloud. 
Next, you will learn about the deployment options on IBM Cloud; this includes topics such as Containers, Kubernetes, and OpenShift.
You will also become familiar with IBM Cloud services such as Databases, Artificial Intelligence and Watson, Blockchain, Internet of Things, and many others.
